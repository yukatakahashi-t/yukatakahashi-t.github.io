About Me

Hello! I'm Takeyoshi, a computer science student at BYUH from Japan. My interests lie in Natural Language Processing and Virtual Experiences. I have a strong passion for exploring the possibilities of these fields and utilizing technology to create innovative solutions.

During my academic journey, I had the opportunity to conduct research on automatic speech recognition. I presented my findings at conferences held in Hawaii and Singapore, allowing me to engage with fellow researchers and industry professionals. Additionally, I have published mobile games on the Android Google Play Store and have gained experience in developing web applications. These experiences have not only honed my technical skills but have also fostered my creativity and problem-solving abilities.

I approach challenges with a positive mindset and thrive in collaborative environments. As I continue my academic journey, I am excited to contribute to the ever-evolving field of computer science. Whether it's developing groundbreaking applications, pushing the boundaries of Natural Language Processing, or creating immersive virtual experiences, I am committed to making a positive impact in the world through technology.

Thank you for visiting my page. If you have any inquiries or would like to collaborate on exciting projects, please feel free to reach out. I look forward to connecting with you!
